<p>I&#8217;ve received this beautiful poem from <a href="https://circleci.com/" target="_blank">CircleCI</a>. thought of sharing it.</p>
<blockquote><p>’Twas the night before Christmas, and all through the startup<br />
were grim engineers who had taken old BART up<br />
to put out a fire that users had found;<br />
they hacked and they Slacked but did not make a sound.</p>
<p>No presents, no stockings, no holiday cheer;<br />
there was only LaCroix and some leftover beer.<br />
Management, too, were awake in their beds,<br />
while visions of bankruptcy danced in their heads.</p>
<p>The bug was quite bad, a security flaw<br />
that was pushed in September, but nobody saw.<br />
“That PR was huge! How could we review it?!”<br />
cried one righteous soul, the one who approved it.</p>
<p>“I explained in the comments that you didn’t read,”<br />
typed the perp and then added a “frown” emoji.<br />
Then out of that chat there arose such a clatter,<br />
I sprang from my desk to see what was the matter.</p></blockquote>
