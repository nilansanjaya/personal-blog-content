<p>Is positive thinking is an illusion? we are to be believed that everything that happens has a positive reason and to move on. I&#8217;m not an expert on the &#8220;Positive Thinking&#8221; topic. even though its a widely spreading fact among people. But i do use the concept, trying to see the positiveness of everything that happens in life, and it actually makes you feel good.  specially when you need a mood fixing, it helps.  I haven&#8217;t had any problems with that so far, but what bothers me is, Is it Real?</p>
<p>Does things really happens for a good reason? Even all the bad happens to you happens for a good reason? for a good cause?</p>
<p>Or are we brainwashing our selves to be fine with what ever happens. So we will stop worrying about the things that&#8217;s happening.</p>
<p>Still trying to figure that out.</p>
