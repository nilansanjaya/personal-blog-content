<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Last week, I reached out to my friends, family, and colleagues, asking for their support in an AI accelerator competition for my business. I had no idea of the <em>wave of support</em> that would follow!</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">I was completely <em>amazed</em> by how everyone went above and beyond to help. <img src="https://s0.wp.com/wp-content/mu-plugins/wpcom-smileys/twemoji/2/72x72/1f496.png" alt="💖" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" /></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>My cousins and colleagues didn’t just vote — they rallied their own friends and families to join in!</li>



<li>My incredible clients shared posts on their social media, urging their networks to support us.</li>



<li>Even people I had only recently connected with stepped up, helping to gather more votes!</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Although we didn’t win the competition, I walked away with something far more valuable, a great understanding of how much the people in your life truly matter.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This experience reminded me that it’s not just about business or networking, it’s about building <em>meaningful and genuine connections</em>. Professional or personal, having a strong, supportive community around you is priceless.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">To everyone who showed up for me: <strong>Thank you.</strong> Your kindness and effort will stay with me, and this is a “win” I’ll always cherish. <img src="https://s0.wp.com/wp-content/mu-plugins/wpcom-smileys/twemoji/2/72x72/1f64f.png" alt="🙏" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" /></p>
