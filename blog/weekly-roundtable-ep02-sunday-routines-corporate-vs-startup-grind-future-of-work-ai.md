<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Another great Weekly Roundtable session today. It’s only our second session, and honestly, it couldn’t have gone better! I think everyone should definitely check it out!</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It’s an unscripted, no-agenda space where amazing professionals from all walks of life come together to talk business, technology, and everything in between.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">What began as a simple Sunday routine check-in quickly evolved into a fascinating conversation about how we all unwind and gear up for the week ahead, from weekend rituals like meal prepping and coffee runs to the real talk on work-life balance.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">We discussed the differences between the corporate 9-to-5 grind and the startup hustle, is startup flexibility really freedom, or just a round-the-clock commitment?</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Then, we dove into the future of work with AI shaking things up, from how “no-code” tools are flooding the market with clone products, to AI’s role in reshaping accountability, SEO, and even entry level jobs.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It’s incredible to see how a casual conversation can open up such important insights and spark fresh ideas.</p>
