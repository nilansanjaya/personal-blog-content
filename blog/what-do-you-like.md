<p>Its a simple question some one asked me, and a simple answer i was going to give, when it took me few seconds to realize that i don&#8217;t have a simple answer. While knowing it&#8217;s for a gift, I was sure it has to be an object or a thing. Something i would use or useful for me.  but what?</p>
<p>There are only few items i use daily. and very few things i consume ( Except Food ). Since most of my days have the same routing between home and office. It only change course once in a while on a weekend or a holiday, and yet, its most unlikely i&#8217;l be using anything extra.  I kept thinking and thinking and end up with nothing specific. But i do have things i like, which randomly comes in to mind or depending on the moment, and also the hardest to take off from the mind!</p>
<p>I suppose that makes the life a little bit more simpler ( or complex? )</p>
